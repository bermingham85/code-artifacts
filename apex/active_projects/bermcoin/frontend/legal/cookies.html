Cookie Policy

Version 1.0 — Bermech Limited, Dublin, Ireland

The short version: Shegoo does not use tracking cookies. We do not run analytics. We do not serve ads. Your browsing is your business.

What We Use Instead

Instead of cookies, Shegoo uses browser localStorage to remember two things:

Item Purpose Duration
Email gate preference Remembers your email so you do not have to re-enter it on the calculator each visit Until you clear your browser data
Cookie consent preference Remembers that you have seen and acknowledged the cookie notice Until you clear your browser data

localStorage is stored entirely in your browser. It is never sent to our servers automatically, and we cannot access it remotely.

What We Do NOT Use

  • No tracking cookies — we do not track your behaviour across pages or sessions.
  • No third-party cookies — no advertising networks, no social media trackers.
  • No analytics cookies — no Google Analytics, no Hotjar, no Mixpanel, nothing.
  • No advertising cookies — we do not serve ads and never will.

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display our typefaces (Playfair Display and DM Sans). When your browser loads these fonts, Google may set a cookie for font caching purposes. This is a standard browser-level behaviour and is used solely to speed up font loading on return visits. We have no control over this cookie. You can learn more at Google Fonts Privacy.

How to Clear localStorage

If you want to remove the data Shegoo stores in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear Browsing Data > tick “Cookies and other site data”
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data > find shegoo.money > Remove

Or simply open your browser’s Developer Tools (F12), go to the Application tab, and delete the localStorage entries for shegoo.money.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our cookie practices, contact us at hello@shegoo.money or visit our contact page.
