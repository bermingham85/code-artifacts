Questions

Does Shegoo transfer money for me?
No. Shegoo is a rate-watching and notification service. When the rate hits your target we nudge you, and you send through your preferred provider (Wise, Revolut, your bank, etc.).
Where do the exchange rates come from?
We use the Frankfurter API, which sources data from the European Central Bank. Rates are updated every working day and interpolated on weekends.
Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. One watch, one route, three nudges per month, no credit card required, no trial period.
Can I change my plan later?
Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
How accurate are the calculator estimates?
The calculator uses live mid-market rates and publicly available fee schedules. Actual delivered amounts depend on your provider, transfer method, and timing. All figures are estimates, not guarantees.
