Getting Started

Bermcoin watches exchange rates for you and sends a notification when your target rate is hit. Think of it as a price alert for currency. We are an information service only — we do not move money or provide financial advice.

Enter your email on the homepage calculator to get started. No password needed — we use a magic link to sign you in. You will have an account within seconds.

Yes. The free plan gives you one rate watch with email notifications. No credit card required, no time limit, no catch. Paid plans add more watches and more notification channels.

From your Dashboard, click “New Watch”. Choose a money route (for example, EUR to GBP), set your target rate, and pick how you want to be notified. That is it — we will do the watching.

Rate Watches

We check rates multiple times per day. The exact frequency depends on your plan and the currency pair. ECB reference rates update once daily around 16:00 CET. Revolut rates update more frequently during market hours.

Our rates are sourced from the European Central Bank (ECB) and Revolut. We display both so you can compare. These are reference rates and may differ slightly from the rate you get when you actually exchange money.

A money route is a currency pair with a direction. For example, “EUR to GBP” is one route and “GBP to EUR” is another. Each route represents the exchange direction you care about.

The free plan includes one active rate watch. The Plus plan gives you up to 5, and the Pro plan gives you up to 20. You can upgrade any time from your Dashboard.

Messages

By default, we send notifications via email. Paid plans can also receive notifications via Telegram and WhatsApp. You choose your preferred channels in your Dashboard settings.

Yes. On paid plans, you can enable multiple notification channels simultaneously. Each watch can have its own channel preference if you like.

A typical notification tells you the currency pair, the current rate, your target rate, and the source (ECB or Revolut). Every notification includes a “not financial advice” disclaimer.

Each plan has a monthly message allowance. If you reach your limit, we will let you know. You can upgrade your plan for more messages, or wait for the next billing cycle when your allowance resets.

Billing

Go to Dashboard > Plan and choose the plan you want. Payment is handled securely by Stripe. You can pay monthly or annually (annual plans save you money).

Go to Dashboard > Plan > Cancel. Your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period. After that, you will revert to the free plan. No questions asked.

Yes. Paid plans renew automatically at the end of each billing period (monthly or annually). You will receive an email before each renewal. Cancel any time to stop future charges.

We do not offer refunds for partial billing periods. If you cancel mid-cycle, you keep access until the period ends. If there is a genuine billing error, contact us and we will sort it out.
