Questions

Does Bermcoin handle my money?

No. Bermcoin is an information service only. We watch exchange rates and tell you when your target rate arrives. You decide when and how to send money using your own provider.

Where do the rates come from?

All rates come from the European Central Bank, published daily via the Frankfurter service. These are the same reference rates used by banks across Europe.

How quickly will I get a message?

Bermcoin checks rates every 15 minutes. When your target rate is reached, you will receive a message within seconds on your chosen channel — Telegram, email, or WhatsApp (Pro).

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel from your dashboard or email us. Your rate watches are saved for 90 days in case you come back.

Is my data safe?

Bermech Limited is an Irish company. We follow GDPR rules. We only store your email and rate preferences. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
